Welcome to the Singapore Regional Branch

We are excited to announce the official kickoff of the Digital Twin Consortium Singapore Regional Branch! This inaugural event marks a significant milestone for the digital twin community in Southeast Asia.

Join us for an afternoon of strategic discussions, networking, and collaborative planning as we establish the foundation for Digital Twin Consortium activities in Singapore and the broader region.

Event Agenda

2:00 PM - Welcome & Introductions

Opening remarks from Sean Whiteley, Regional Branch Organizer, and introduction to the Digital Twin Consortium mission and vision for Southeast Asia.

2:30 PM - DTC Global Update

Dan Isaacs, CTO of Digital Twin Consortium, joins remotely to share updates on global DTC initiatives, industry trends, and the strategic importance of the Singapore Regional Branch.

3:15 PM - Charter Review

Review and discussion of the Singapore Regional Branch charter, governance structure, and member engagement framework. This session will establish the operational foundation for our regional activities.

3:45 PM - Refreshment Break & Networking

Take a break, connect with fellow digital twin enthusiasts, and explore collaboration opportunities.

4:15 PM - 2026 Initiative Planning

Interactive session to identify and prioritize key initiatives for 2026, including:

  • Industry verticals and use cases relevant to Singapore and Southeast Asia
  • Working group formations and focus areas
  • Event calendar and community engagement plans
  • Partnership and collaboration opportunities

4:45 PM - Member Updates: Singapore Digital Twin Initiatives

Brief presentations from members on ongoing and planned digital twin projects in Singapore, showcasing real-world implementations and lessons learned.

5:00 PM - Closing Remarks & Next Steps

Summary of action items, upcoming milestones, and how to stay engaged with the Singapore Regional Branch.

Who Should Attend

This event is ideal for:

  • Digital Twin Consortium members in Singapore and Southeast Asia
  • Organizations interested in joining the Digital Twin Consortium
  • Technology providers and solution integrators
  • End-user organizations exploring digital twin adoption
  • Government and academic institutions
  • Industry associations and ecosystem partners

Event Format

This is a hybrid event. Attendees can participate in person at our Singapore venue or join virtually via our online platform. Details will be shared upon registration.

About the Speakers

Sean Whiteley is the founder of Axomem and the Regional Branch Organizer for the Digital Twin Consortium Singapore. With deep expertise in digital twin technology and enterprise software, Sean leads the effort to build the DTC community in Southeast Asia.

PK Gupta is Vice President and General Manager at Dell Technologies, bringing extensive experience in enterprise technology, edge computing, and digital transformation strategies to the digital twin ecosystem.

Dan Isaacs serves as the Chief Technology Officer of the Digital Twin Consortium globally. Dan provides strategic direction on digital twin standards, interoperability, and industry adoption, and will join the Singapore kickoff remotely from the United States.
